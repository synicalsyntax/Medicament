Medicament

A marketplace for medical supplies commerce

Hospitals constantly dispose unused or unnecessary medical equipment once they receive new medical equipment, though the old equipment is still salvageable. Hospitals also accumulate surplus supplies due to strict return policies. Medicament intends to use these surplus supplies to help other hospitals by creating a network of hospitals. With Medicament, hospitals can help each other by donating and trading supplies.
