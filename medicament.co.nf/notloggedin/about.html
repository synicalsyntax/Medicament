Medicament Inc.

Medicament Inc., a team of six high-school students, developed Medicament for an interdisciplinary project in which the group was challenged to mitigate a real-world problem in six weeks.

Medicament Inc. created this platform to aid an organization known as Doctors Without Borders, an organization that provides medical care in third-world countries; they discovered that their invention was also applicable in the United States.

Medicament Inc. hopes to improve the future of healthcare and the environment in hospitals by promoting communication and the exchange of supplies.
